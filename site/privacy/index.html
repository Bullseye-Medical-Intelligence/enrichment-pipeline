1. Who We Are

Bullseye Medical Intelligence is a physician targeting research service operated by Rajiv Thind and Osama Aziz ("we," "us," or "our"). We provide commercial intelligence services to healthcare sales and marketing teams, including medical device companies, specialty therapeutics companies, diagnostics providers, and other specialty healthcare commercial organizations.

Our website is located at bullseyemedical.ai. Our primary contact for privacy-related inquiries is leads@bullseyemedical.ai.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you submit a form on our website, we collect the information you enter, which may include:

  • First and last name
  • Work email address
  • Company or organization name
  • Product or therapy area description
  • Any additional information you voluntarily include in a message

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source (for example, a social media platform)
  • General geographic region based on IP address

This information is used solely for understanding how visitors interact with our website and improving the experience. It is not linked to your identity.

Information from commercial engagements

When organizations engage Bullseye as a commercial client, we may collect business contact information including names, email addresses, and role titles of designated team members for the purpose of delivering our services.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form
  • To schedule and conduct discovery calls with prospective clients
  • To deliver research services to commercial clients
  • To communicate about our services, including sending follow-up information you have requested
  • To improve our website and better understand how visitors engage with our content
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your information for automated decision-making, profiling, or behavioral advertising.

Legal bases for processing (EEA/UK)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process personal information under one or more of these legal bases:

  • Consent — where you have given clear consent, such as submitting a form.
  • Legitimate interests — to operate and improve our website, respond to inquiries, conduct physician-targeting research from public sources, and run our business, where those interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Contract — to deliver services to clients who have engaged us.
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable law.

4. Data We Do Not Use

Bullseye Medical Intelligence provides research services based exclusively on publicly observable, commercially permissible signals. Our research methodology does not involve the collection, processing, or use of the following:

Never collected or used in any capacity
  • Patient-level data of any kind
  • Protected health information (PHI) as defined under HIPAA
  • Medical records or clinical records
  • Insurance claims files or claims data
  • Appointment or scheduling data
  • Electronic medical record (EMR) or electronic health record (EHR) data
  • Data from login-gated systems or paywalled databases
  • Private or proprietary databases requiring licensed access

Our research draws exclusively from publicly observable physician and practice-level signals, including publicly listed services, directories, business listings, and patient-facing web content. Every data point used in our research is sourced from information that is intentionally made public by the practice or physician.

5. Information About Physicians and Practices in Our Research

Bullseye's core service analyzes the publicly available, professional online footprint of physicians and medical practices. This research may include information that identifies a healthcare professional in their professional capacity — for example, a physician's name, practice affiliation, specialty, publicly listed services, and practice location.

We collect this information only from sources the practice or physician has intentionally made public, such as practice websites, public business listings, professional directories, and patient-facing web content. We never use any of the categories listed in Section 4. Where this information constitutes personal data under applicable law, our legal basis is the legitimate interest of Bullseye and our clients in lawful commercial research, balanced against the limited, professional, and already-public nature of the information.

If you are a physician or practice representative and would like to access, correct, or request removal of information about you from our research, email leads@bullseyemedical.ai. We honor applicable requests as described in Section 10.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers

We use a small number of third-party services to operate our website and manage communications. These currently include:

  • Formspree — form submission processing. Information submitted through our contact form is transmitted to Formspree servers for delivery to our team.
  • Calendly — meeting scheduling. If you book a call, the information you provide to Calendly is subject to Calendly's own privacy policy.

These service providers are authorized to use your information only to perform services on our behalf and are not permitted to use it for any other purpose.

Legal requirements

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, or investigate fraud.

Business transfers

In the event that Bullseye Medical Intelligence is acquired, merged, or transferred, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website if such a transfer occurs.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses minimal tracking technology. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or behavioral retargeting tools.

We may use basic analytics to understand aggregate visitor behavior, such as which pages are most visited and where visitors arrive from. This data is not linked to personally identifiable information.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not affect your ability to access or use our website.

8. Data Retention

We retain information submitted through our contact form for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and manage our client relationship, typically no longer than 24 months from last contact.

If you are a commercial client, we retain business contact information for the duration of our engagement and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes.

You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us at leads@bullseyemedical.ai.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. Form submissions are processed through Formspree, which maintains its own security controls.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to use commercially reasonable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have some or all of the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Access. Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction. Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion. Request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Portability. Request your information in a commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction and objection. Ask us to restrict, or object to, certain uses of your information.
  • Withdraw consent. Where we rely on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting processing already carried out.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at leads@bullseyemedical.ai. We will verify your request using the email address associated with it and respond within the period required by law (generally 30 to 45 days). We do not discriminate against anyone for exercising their privacy rights.

Additional rights in the EEA and UK (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. We would appreciate the chance to address your concerns directly first.

Additional rights for California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request its deletion or correction, and to opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information, and have not done so in the preceding 12 months. We do not process sensitive personal information for purposes that would give rise to a right to limit, and we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

In the preceding 12 months, we may have collected the following categories of personal information, for the business purposes in Section 3 and from the sources in Section 2:

  • Identifiers — such as name, work email, and IP address.
  • Commercial information — such as the services or product areas you inquire about.
  • Internet or network activity — such as how you interact with our website.
  • Geolocation data — general region derived from IP address.
  • Professional or employment information — such as company name and job title.

You or your authorized agent may submit a request by emailing leads@bullseyemedical.ai.
