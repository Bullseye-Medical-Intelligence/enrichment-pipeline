How We Rank Targets

Scoring Methodology

Every account is evaluated against a structured ICP signal checklist drawn entirely from publicly available web data. Each signal requires a direct textual anchor — no assumptions, no inferred data. The result is a two-axis verdict: tier (the call priority) and confidence band (the evidence depth). Internal numeric scores stay in the data layer and are never included in client-facing output.

Bullseye

All must-have signals confirmed. Highest-priority accounts with strong clinical and commercial fit. Ready for outreach.

Contender

Solid fit with one or more unverified items. Worth calling, but a discovery question may shift the priority. Good for pipeline building.

Excluded

Disqualified by a structural rule before any enrichment spend. Excluded accounts are shown here so the decision logic is transparent.
