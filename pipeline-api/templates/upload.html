What happens after you upload

  1. Your CSV is validated and the roster is loaded. No crawl or LLM spend yet.
  2. You are taken to the roster, where you review the list and pre-excluded records.
  3. When ready, click Enrich All to start the pipeline. Results appear as the run completes.
  4. Enrichment takes roughly 2-4 minutes for 10 records, 10-20 minutes for 50 records, and 45-90 minutes for 200 records.
